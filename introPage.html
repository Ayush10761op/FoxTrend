ABOUT

We are a team of passionate computer science students, all set to begin our second year of studies. Our group is composed of two skilled frontend developers, a versatile fullstack developer with a strong focus on backend, and a dedicated machine learning engineer. What brought us together is our shared love for programming, development, and all things tech. Our journey began in the classroom, where we quickly realized that we shared not just a timetable but a deep passion for coding. It wasn’t long before we decided to channel that enthusiasm into something tangible—a full-scale web application, which also marks our first collaborative project. Our mission is simple: to gain hands-on experience in building and maintaining a fully functional web application, while also learning the nuances of teamwork and collaboration. Through this project, we aim to not only hone our technical skills but also understand the dynamics of working together as a cohesive unit. Looking ahead, our goal is to refine our skills, learn from the feedback we receive, and continuously improve our application. This project is just the beginning of our journey into the world of software development, and we’re excited to see where it takes us.
